Women Who Changed the World of Technology



“Girls are capable of doing everything men are capable of doing. Sometimes they have more imagination than men”
- Katherine Johnson, NASA Mathematician

Throughout history, numerous women have made significant contributions to the field of technology, often breaking down barriers and over coming societal biases. Their pioneering work has shaped and transformed the technological landscape we know today. Here are a few remarkable women who have changed the face of technology.

Ada Lovelace

Mathematician and Computer Scientist

Annie Easley

Computer Programmer, NASA

Grace Hopper

Computer Scientist & Mathematician

Hedy Lamarr

"The Mother of WiFi"
